Registration to the Workshop is Open!!


All participants are welcome to register to the workshop completing the online registration!

  The confirmation of your registration will be sent by the organisers by 15 Oct 2021. You are not fully registered until after your registration request has been confirmed. Please do not make any binding travel arrangements before your registration request has been confirmed. In case of a large number of registered participants, a selection will be made to ensure a geographical balance of the countries represented. Priority will be given to ESS participants.

  All participants from non-Schengen zone countries attending the meeting are requested to have a valid passport and, if required, a visa. Applications for visas should be made at the Embassy of Spain, with reference to the ESS Workshop on the use of administrative data and social statistics. For accepted participants, if necessary, Devstat can provide a letter to facilitate obtaining a visa.

All participants are welcome to register to workshop completing the online registration below:

  All registrations should be made by 15 Oct 2021.

  Practical matters:

  The travel expenses for one delegate per EU Member State will be financed by Eurostat through DEVSTAT, Servicios de Consultoría Estadística, according to the European Commission rules on the reimbursement of expenses incurred by people from outside the Commission invited to attend meetings in an expert capacity. Please note that the reimbursement does not include accommodation and per diem.

As to comply with the needed procedural requirements, each delegate should provide:

  • A copy of passport

  • The original boarding pass(es) / train ticket.
